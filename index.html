About Me & My Work

With over 30 years of experience in the masonry trade, I have dedicated my life to the art and craft of working with stone, brick, and block. My passion for creating durable and beautiful structures is evident in every project I undertake.

I specialize in a wide range of masonry services, including intricate brickwork, making caps, robust stone foundations, elegant fireplaces, durable patio pillars, custom outdoor living spaces, and much more! My commitment to quality, attention to detail, and deep understanding of traditional and modern masonry techniques ensure that every project is built to last and exceeds client expectations.

I believe in honest work, clear communication, and delivering results that stand the test of time, both aesthetically and structurally.
